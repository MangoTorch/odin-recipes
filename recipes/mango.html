3 Ingredient Mango Ice cream

Description

Make mango ice cream by blending diced mangoes, mixing with whipped heavy cream and sugar, and freezing for 24 hours with high speed blender or 8 hours in a container. It's a taste of the tropics in every scoop.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cupsmango puree (from 4 to 5 mangos)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup white sugar

Directions

  1. Peel and dice mangoes. Place in a blender and blend until pureed; measure out 1 1/2 cups puree; set aside. Refrigerate remaining puree for another use.
  2. Whip heavy cream and sugar with an electric mixer on high speed until soft peaks form. Reduce the mixer speed to low and mix in mango puree.
  3. Pour into containers or into a freezer-safe bowl. Freeze for 24 hours if using high speed blender or for 8 hours to overnight if freezing in a container.
  4. Following manufacturers instructions, insert the pint container into the outer bowl of high speed blender. Push “Ice Cream” button. If freezing in a container, remove from the freezer and let sit for 8 to 10 minutes or until soft enough to scoop.
