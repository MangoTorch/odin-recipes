Nutella Ice Cream

Description

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this easy-to-make Nutella ice cream. This homemade delight combines the creamy goodness of Nutella with just three simple ingredients: heavy cream, sweetened condensed milk, and, of course, Nutella. Blend them, freeze, and voilà! You'll have a rich and velvety ice cream that's a chocolate lover's dream. Perfect for a quick, indulgent treat anytime you're in need of a sweet pick-me-up.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream, divided
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup cocoa hazelnut spread (such as Nutella®)
  • 1 ounce hazelnuts, crushed, or as needed

Steps

  1. Combine milk, cream, and 1/2 cup sugar in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook until sugar dissolves, about 5 minutes.
  2. Beat egg yolks with the remaining sugar in a bowl with an electric mixer until eggs become thick and pale yellow, about 4 minutes.
  3. Pour 1/2 cup warm milk mixture into egg mixture and stir. Add this mixture back into the saucepan. Cook over very low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture becomes thick enough to coat the back of a wooden spoon, 7 to 10 minutes.
  4. Place a strainer over a bowl and pour the warm custard mixture through the strainer. Stir in vanilla and hazelnut spread until it dissolves.
  5. Chill mixture completely, about 1 hour. Pour into an ice cream maker and churn until frozen according to manufacturer’s instructions, 15 to 20 minutes.
  6. Transfer to a plastic container and place in the freezer for an hour before serving. Top with crushed hazelnuts for serving.
